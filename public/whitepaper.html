LCM Coin

Liquid Crypto Money — Future of Digital Transactions

1. Introduction

LCM (Liquid Crypto Money) is a digital coin system designed to reduce global transaction costs, increase speed, and provide decentralized mining rewards.

2. Problem

  • High banking fees
  • Slow international payments
  • Limited financial access

3. Solution

LCM provides a mining-based ecosystem where users earn coins through activity and referrals.

4. Mining Plan

LCM mining is designed for a 2-year controlled distribution cycle to ensure stability and fairness.

5. Roadmap

Phase 1

System development and early mining launch.

Phase 2

User expansion and mining stabilization.

Phase 2.5 (KYC)

Identity verification phase (3–5 months extension for compliance and security).

Phase 3

Mining control and ecosystem optimization.

Phase 25–26

Public launch on exchanges through strategic partnerships.

6. Vision

LCM aims to become a global decentralized payment and mining ecosystem with real-world adoption.
